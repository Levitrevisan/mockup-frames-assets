Mockup Frames

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-16

Mockup Frames is a Google Slides™ add-on that wraps images on your slides in device frames (phones, tablets, laptops, watches, and browsers). This policy explains what data the add-on accesses, how we protect it, and what we never do.

Data we access

The add-on requests the minimum OAuth scopes needed to do its job. The table below lists every scope, the data it touches, and how that data is protected.

OAuth scopeWhat it accessesHow it is protected
presentations.currentonly (sensitive) Only the single presentation you currently have open — the image you select and the frame + image we write back into that slide. Access is limited to the open document; the add-on cannot see your other files or Google Drive™. Content is processed transiently to complete the action and is never stored by us.
script.container.ui Displays the add-on's sidebar inside Google Slides™. Accesses no presentation content or personal data.
script.external_request Fetches the static device-frame images from our own domain (mockupframes.levitrevisan.com). These requests are outbound only, sent over HTTPS, and contain no presentation content, image data, or personal information.

How we protect your data

  • Encryption in transit — All communication between the add-on, Google's servers, and our asset domain uses HTTPS/TLS. Data is never transmitted over unencrypted connections.
  • Transient, in-memory processing — The image you select is read and re-inserted only for the duration of the action you trigger. It is held in memory only as long as needed to complete that operation and is then discarded. Nothing is written to a database or any persistent store.
  • No transmission to us or to third parties — Your presentation content and images stay within Google's infrastructure and your browser session. They are never sent to our servers or to any third party. Our only outbound requests retrieve static frame images from our own domain and include none of your data.
  • Scoped, least-privilege access — We request only the scopes above. The presentations.currentonly scope restricts the add-on to the presentation you have open; it cannot access your other documents or Google Drive™.
  • No accounts or profiles — We do not create user accounts, store identifiers, or build usage profiles. The add-on does not request your email address.

What we never do

  • We never read your presentations beyond the action you request.
  • We never store your images or presentation content on our servers.
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We never use your Google user data to train, develop, or improve generalized or non-personalized AI and/or machine-learning models.

Data retention and deletion

Because the add-on processes data transiently and keeps no database of users or content, there is no stored personal data to retain or delete. If you stop using the add-on, you can remove it at any time from Extensions → Add-ons → Manage add-ons in Google Slides™, which revokes its access. Questions about data handling can be sent to us via the Support page.

Pricing

Mockup Frames is free to use. We do not collect any payment information and no payment processor is involved. If we introduce optional paid features in the future, this policy will be updated before any additional data (such as an email address to identify a plan) is requested or handled.

Google API Services disclosure

Mockup Frames' use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, note them on this page before they take effect.
