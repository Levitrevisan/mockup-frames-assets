Mockup Frames

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-14

What we collect

  • Images you choose — screenshots you upload or select on a slide are processed transiently in your browser to insert them into your presentation. They are never sent to or stored by us.
  • Google account email — the add-on requests the userinfo.email OAuth scope. Mockup Frames is currently free, and we do not store your email, build a profile, or track your usage. If optional paid features are added later, your email would be used only to identify your plan, and this policy would be updated first.

What we never do

  • We never read your presentations beyond the action you request (scope presentations.currentonly limits access to the open document).
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We never store your images or presentation content on our servers.

Pricing

Mockup Frames is free to use. We do not collect any payment information and no payment processor is involved. If we introduce optional paid features in the future, this policy will be updated before any payment data is handled.

Data retention and deletion

While the add-on is free we do not keep a database of users. If future account features store any data associated with your email, you can request its deletion by contacting us at the address below.

Google API Services disclosure

Mockup Frames' use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
