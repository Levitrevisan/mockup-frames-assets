Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-13

What we collect

  • Google account email — used only to identify your subscription status. Requested via the userinfo.email OAuth scope.
  • Subscription status — stored in a private datastore when you subscribe to Pro, associated with your email.
  • Images you choose — screenshots you upload, pick from Google Drive (only files you explicitly select, via the drive.file scope) or select on a slide are processed transiently to insert them into your presentation. They are never stored by us.

What we never do

  • We never read your presentations beyond the action you request (scope presentations.currentonly limits access to the open document).
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We never store your images or presentation content on our servers.

Payments

Pro subscriptions are processed by Stripe. Your card details are handled exclusively by Stripe and never reach us. Stripe shares with us only your email and subscription status. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Data retention and deletion

Subscription records are kept while your subscription is active and for up to 12 months after cancellation for accounting purposes. To request deletion of your data, contact us at the address below.

Google API Services disclosure

Mockup Frames' use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
